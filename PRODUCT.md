# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Sydney Shibari community members, English-speaking, scanning a QR code from a physical bamboo inscribed with bronze characters. They encounter the page in a social/community context — curious about what the bamboo says and why it was left.

## Product Purpose

Explain the meaning and context of a bronze inscription (金文) carved into a bamboo donated to the Sydney Shibari community. The page translates and interprets the ancient text, bridging a personal farewell gift and a shared community space.

## Positioning

A bamboo carries words cast three thousand years apart — an ancient blessing form offered to a modern community.

## Brand Personality

克制 (restrained) · 温润 (warm/gentle) · 有雅 (refined/elegant)

## Anti-references

None specific.

## Design Principles

- **Let the inscription lead.** The characters are the focal point; layout and pacing serve the text, not decoration.
- **Restrained elegance.** Every element earns its place. Nothing excessive, nothing ornamental without purpose.
- **Context-aware.** Speaks to a Shibari community audience with warmth and respect, not a sterile museum display.
- **Calm confidence.** Dark, quiet, grounded — the page doesn't shout.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Semantic HTML structure
- Readable contrast ratios
- Reduced motion respected
- Screen-reader-friendly labels for inscription characters
